# Career Optimization Agent Overview

This agent is designed to help individuals navigate their career paths by providing personalized assistance in job hunting and professional development.

## Key Features
- Tailored job recommendations based on user profiles.
- Comprehensive resume building and editing tools.
- Mock interview setups and feedback.
- Alerts for new job postings and changes in job markets.

## How to Use
1. Sign up or log in.
2. Fill out the career profile questionnaire.
3. Access your personalized dashboard for recommendations and resources.
4. Utilize provided tools for improving your resume and interview skills.

Together, we will optimize your career journey!
