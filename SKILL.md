# Career Optimization Agent

## Purpose
This skill intelligently prepares users for optimal job opportunities based on their resume, preferences, GitHub projects, and more.

## Features
- **Input Analysis**: Evaluates resumes, skills, and preferences to provide tailored job insights.
- **Verification**: Confirms users’ skills through assessments or project reviews.
- **Resume Preparation**: Creates or refines resumes to align with targeted job descriptions.
- **Interview Preparation**: Offers coaching and common questions related to specific jobs and industries.
- **Notification Automation**: Sends alerts for relevant job openings and application deadlines.

## Workflow
1. **User Input**: Collect information via a form or chatbot interface.
2. **Analysis**: Use algorithms to analyze skills and preferences.
3. **Recommendations**: Generate job recommendations tailored to user qualifications.
4. **Resources**: Provide links to helpful resources (resume templates, interview tips, etc.).
5. **Feedback Loop**: Collect user feedback to improve the system.

## Technologies
- **Data Analytics**: To assess resumes and job preferences.
- **Machine Learning**: For improving recommendation accuracy.
- **Notification System**: To keep users updated on opportunities.
